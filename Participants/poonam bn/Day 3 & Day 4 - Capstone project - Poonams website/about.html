About Us

Who are we?

TidalFood

More than a food shop. The food we make here is a family-owned shop that focuses on chef-made, small-batch, craft food that rotate based on seasonality. We're a 100% scratch bakery, so we use nothing but the highest quality ingredients and making everything in house! Naturally, we don’t use any artificial ingredients in anything we make, because, well - that’s gross! If we wouldn’t serve it to our own family, we definitely wouldn’t serve it to yours! All of our yeast-raised food are based on our 24hr Brioche recipe which is buttery, insanely flavorful and fluffy. Our cake food are a little crunchy on the outside, soft & moist on the inside and from time to time we'll even fold fresh fruit and other things into them! Coffee? Yep, we've got you covered. We’ve worked incredibly hard and poured every ounce of heart and passion we have into pushing the boundaries of what a 'food' is, to make sure we’re not only Chennai’s best food shop, but one of the best food shops in the state.
